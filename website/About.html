Rural Development Scheme

About the Scheme

The Rural Development Scheme is a government initiative aimed at improving the living standards and infrastructure in rural areas. It focuses on various aspects such as:

  • Providing access to clean water and sanitation facilities
  • Developing rural roads and transportation networks
  • Promoting agriculture and rural livelihoods
  • Enhancing education and healthcare services
  • Empowering local communities through skill development and employment opportunities

This scheme is implemented with the collaboration of local governing bodies, NGOs, and community participation to ensure sustainable development in rural regions.
